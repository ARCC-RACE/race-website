R.A.C.E. provides after school educational programs and competitions to teach about Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

Using the AWS DeepRacer autonomous car, our programs teach students how to build their own AI model and run it on the car. We host tournaments that bring together students tot race for the fastest autonomous time around a track..

We are an organization committed to teaching the next generation of innovators about Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence

R.A.C.E. is a project by the Autonomous Race Car Community (ARCC) to bring educational experiences to students using the AWS DeepRacer
